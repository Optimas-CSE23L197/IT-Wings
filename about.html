About us

| What is IT-WINGS about?

We are convinced that the only way to hold one’s own in an ever faster networked world is to constantly change and expand one’s own knowledge and skills. That is why we continually educate ourselves. We regularly keep ourselves informed about the latest tools and trends in performance marketing as well as changes in the market.

This way, we can provide you with well-founded and at the same time up-to-date approaches that guarantee you a decisive competitive advantage. We see challenges as an opportunity to gain experience, optimize processes and continuously develop ourselves so that we can provide you with the best possible benefit.

| Why IT-WINGS?

Go beyond just fulfilling your customer wishes.

IT-WINGS combines scientific methods with web analytics. This unique approach ensures that the usual web analytics tools are not sufficient for us to handle your projects. Therefore, we develop company- and industry-specific algorithms to help your e-commerce projects and big data challenges.

  • Client-specific analytics and consulting
  • Scientific view of your data
  • Developing unique tools and predictive solutions
  • Training your employees in complex web analytics

Our strategy

In order to realize our desired customer satisfaction, we will continue to train in the core fields of digital analytics, performance marketing and data science. Given our targeted approach, you’ll quickly have a high level of commitment, and we can work with you in matters of effectiveness and efficiency in the long term.
